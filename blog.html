What is VerveIN?
VerveIN

VerveIN (innovations) is an educational technology company dedicated to transforming traditional learning through immersive virtual reality (VR) experiences. By offering a comprehensive suite of VR solutions tailored for K-12 schools, colleges, and universities, VerveIN bridges the gap between conventional education and advanced technology, making cutting-edge educational experiences accessible to all students.

The Problem We Solve
VerveIN

What if educators wanted to improve the way they could teach students? And their new big idea is Virtual Reality: an innovative approach to learning and other aspects of life. But an educator would find numerous challenges to overcome, a lack of content library, high costs of having their own content development team, and a high skill set for developing content and setting up the tech. Implementing VR in education is a lot more challenging, and just think, how much trouble they would have to go through just to set up their new way of teaching, the time they could spend on improving their lessons. But why would an educator even feel the need to improve the current way of teaching? Lack of attention span, the depressed mindset of students regarding learning, and the rise of digital solutions which are proved to be better than traditional learning. All these problems serve as the main motivation behind VerveIN.

The Solution We Provide
VerveIN

VerveIN provides a platform where educators can find everything they need in one place, from setting up VR tech in their classroom, to VR headsets, and a content library that would make learning so much better for their students, and all that at a very reasonable price. We strive to make learning better and more effective by using Virtual reality. Here is how we tackle each problem: We partner with device partners for mobile phones and buy all the VR headsets, we have our own content development team to develop the VR content according to educators' needs, our onsite team then delivers the service to the school/college at their designated time.
